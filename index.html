Success Stories

Hear from our graduates working in top companies worldwide

Iresha Subasinghe

QC Engineer (Former Document Controller), Dubai

"As a civil engineering graduate on a visit visa, I found myself in a critical situation, running out of time to find employment. Meeting Mr. Viraj from the SL DC Academy was a significant turning point. He provided invaluable advice and guidance, suggesting a strategic career shift to become a Document Controller as a way to enter the construction industry.

In a very short period, he equipped me with the fundamental knowledge required for the role and provided exceptional support to help me prepare for interviews. Thanks to his mentorship, I successfully secured a position as a Document Controller at a construction company. His professional support didn't end there; Mr. Viraj continued to assist me with any challenges or questions I faced in my new role.

This foundational experience proved to be a fantastic stepping stone. After just over a year, I earned an internal promotion to QC Engineer, aligning with my original career goals. The Document Controller path is an excellent gateway into the construction field, allowing one to grow towards their ambitions or build a professional career in an office environment.

I wholeheartedly recommend the SL DC Academy to anyone looking to launch their career and build a bright future. Their guidance is truly transformative."

Hiruni N.

Cost Control Engineer (Former Document Controller)

"After earning my bachelor's degree in multimedia, I faced a significant setback in my first role as a multimedia officer at UAE. While searching for a new opportunity, I connected with Mr. Viraj for career advice. Following his insightful recommendation, I decided to pivot my career and train to become a Document Controller (DC).

Despite having no prior knowledge of the field, Mr. Viraj's teaching methods were so effective that I was able to grasp the material in a very short time. He also provided thorough guidance on how to approach interviews, which gave me the confidence I needed. I was thrilled to pass my interview and be selected for the job.

Even after I started, Mr. Viraj continued to support me as I navigated an unfamiliar workload and my daily tasks. His mentorship was instrumental. After about a year in the role, I was promoted to Cost Control Engineer. I believe this success was possible because he guided me to make the right decision at the right time.

For anyone who feels uncertain about their career path or is looking for a solid starting point, I highly recommend the SL DC Academy. It’s a place that can set you on the path to success. Highly recommended!"
